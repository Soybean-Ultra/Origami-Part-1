Interesting Facts About Camels

  • Their humps store fat which they live off of for weeks and even months!
  • They can drink up to 40 gallons of water in a single go, and store it.
  • Camels spit as a means to distract whatever they think is a threat.

Interesting Facts About Pandas

  • Their main diet is bamboo.
  • They even spend 10-16 hours eating!
  • A panda's fur is great camoflauge for both the shade and snowy habitats.
